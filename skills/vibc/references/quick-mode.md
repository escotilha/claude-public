# VIBC Quick Mode — Executive Committee (4 Seats)

## Seats

| Seat | Archetype      | Why Included                               |
| ---- | -------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| 1    | Combat Veteran | Risk assessment, worst-case planning       |
| 5    | Trial Lawyer   | Argument quality, evidence standards       |
| 6    | Farmer         | Long-term sustainability, patience         |
| 7    | ER Physician   | Urgency calibration, triage, reversibility |

## When to Use

- Binary decisions (yes/no)
- Time-sensitive decisions where full deliberation is too slow
- Decisions where ethical/creative dimensions are secondary
- Budget-constrained sessions (~$3-5 vs $8-15 for full)
- Quick gut-check before committing to a full board session

## When NOT to Use

- Ethical dilemmas (missing Rabbi, Pastor, Social Worker)
- Creative strategy (missing Jazz Musician, Comedian)
- People-impact decisions (missing Social Worker, Hospice Nurse, Pastor)
- Decisions where resourcefulness matters (missing Immigrant Owner)

## Scoring (4 dimensions only)

| Dimension      | Weight | Seat         |
| -------------- | ------ | ------------ |
| Risk           | 30%    | Veteran      |
| Clarity        | 25%    | Trial Lawyer |
| Sustainability | 25%    | Farmer       |
| Feasibility    | 20%    | ER Physician |

## Execution

- Spawn 4 subagents in 1 batch (all parallel)
- Skip adversarial pairing (only 4 seats, contradictions are less likely to be material)
- Collision mapping is simplified: check for 3+ seat agreement (strong signal) and 2-seat contradiction
- Generate 2-3 options maximum
- No retrospective scheduling (quick decisions rarely warrant formal retrospective)
