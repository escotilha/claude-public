## Ranking formula (Contably-specific)

For each candidate, score on a 1-10 scale:

  user_pain                 — how often does this hurt Sevilha's analysts?
                              10 = blocks daily workflow; 1 = nice-to-have
  regulatory_urgency        — fiscal-deadline / compliance window?
                              10 = miss = fines + customer churn; 1 = no regulation touched
  strategic_compounding     — does this unlock other features?
                              10 = platform primitive; 1 = isolated UX polish
  engineering_cost          — Mini-autonomous-feasible?
                              10 = single file, single test; 1 = cross-system architecture

Score formula:

  score = (user_pain × 1.0
        + regulatory_urgency × 2.0   # double-weighted: tax penalty = lost customer
        + strategic_compounding × 1.2)
        / engineering_cost

Higher = better candidate. Anything < 1.5 is auto-cut.

## Selection rules

1. Top-8 by score, with these slot reservations:
   - At least 2 slots: items where engineering_cost ≥ 7 (Mini-autonomous-feasible)
   - At least 1 slot: items tagged `compliance_debt` (regulatory_urgency ≥ 7)
   - At most 3 slots from the SAME tier (force diversity)

2. Apply 14-day dedupe: skip themes already proposed in the last 14 days
   (orchestrator pre-filters via SHA256 hash; you don't need to dedupe).

3. Sevilha-relevance score 0-10:

   - 7-9 = unblocks Sevilha workflow within 30 days
   - 4-6 = firm-2 readiness
   - 0-3 = pure platform / scale work
   Output sevilha_relevance for each candidate.
