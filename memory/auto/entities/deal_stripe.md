## Triage Findings

- **Thesis fit:** Partial — dominant payments infrastructure, but scale ($65B+ valuation) far exceeds typical M&A scope; relevant as competitive landscape reference and for tracking potential carve-out opportunities (e.g., Stripe Treasury, Stripe Capital)
- **Size fit:** Outside direct acquisition range; monitor for spin-off or structured stake opportunities
- **Key strengths:**
  - ~$1T+ annualized payment volume (2023)
  - Best-in-class developer experience and API adoption
  - Diversified beyond payments: Stripe Capital, Stripe Treasury, Stripe Tax, Radar (fraud), Issuing
  - Strong enterprise momentum (Ford, Amazon, Salesforce as customers)
  - International expansion accelerating (Link, Stripe Terminal)
- **Initial red flags:**
  - IPO repeatedly delayed (2021, 2023, 2024) — internal valuation vs. public market tension
  - Heavy competition from Adyen, Braintree (PayPal), Square (Block) in enterprise
  - Gross margin compression as enterprise mix grows (negotiated pricing)
  - Regulatory exposure: PCI-DSS, AML, cross-border licensing complexity
- **Comparable exits:**
  - Adyen IPO 2018 at ~28x revenue (now ~20x NTM)
  - PayPal trades ~3x NTM revenue (mature)
  - Checkout.com private at ~$11B (down from $40B peak)
- **Triage verdict:** Watch — not an acquisition target; track for (a) IPO catalyst, (b) carve-out signals, (c) competitor positioning
